FAQ

Is this legal?? Like, taking stuff from CodeHS?

Yep! Check out the Fair Use Statement

Do you track users?

I've got a custom page analytics script based on Matomo. It only records the following information:

  • Someone viewed a page
  • The address of the page that was viewed
  • How quickly the page loaded
  • What browser/OS you used
  • Your general location (i.e. region of the country)
Your IP address is not saved, and no cookies are used. I don't sell your data. No personally identifiable information is recorded, ever. I don't want your information. Keep that stuff to yourself, man :/

My teacher gave me a challenge that isn't on here-- huh?

Some teachers have implemented their own challenges on CodeHS. I don't have those unique challenges, sorry; I'd be glad to help you with them, though!

If you have a question that Dinner Coding Time can't answer, please ask it on the Discord! I might not be able to answer it right away, but someone should be able to help you ASAP.
