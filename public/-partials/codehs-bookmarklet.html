CodeHS Bookmarklet

A bookmarklet is a special bookmark that automatically runs a small snippet of code when you click it. This bookmarklet magically gets the solutions to CodeHS problems and inserts them!
All credit for the code goes to ExplosiveNight on the Discord

To add it to your browser, follow the instructions below. To use it, go to any CodeHS assignment page and click on its icon in your favorites bar.

CodeHS Bookmarklet Link

Please note that this is not an intended feature of CodeHS. It might get removed at any time. In addition, please be responsible with this power :) We trust you to handle your learning best, but make sure that you understand the concepts behind the annoying problems that CodeHS builds.

Some solutions have been written incorrectly. Sadly, we can't fix them, as it's on CodeHS's end. In general, the problem is just a typo or a missing word, but if you need any help, please contact us via email or Discord!

Chrome

On Chrome, drag the CodeHS Bookmarklet Link above into your bookmark bar. If you don't see the bookmark bar, you can press CTRL + SHIFT + B to open it.


Edge

On Chrome, drag the CodeHS Bookmarklet Link above into your favorites bar. If you don't see the favorites bar, you can press CTRL + SHIFT + B to open it.

Firefox

On Firefox, drag the CodeHS Bookmarklet Link above into your favorites bar. If you don't see the favorites bar, you can press CTRL + SHIFT + B to open it.
